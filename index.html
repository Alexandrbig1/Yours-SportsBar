About us

Welcome to Yours Sports Bar, the heart of sports scene!‍



Step into our lively sports bar in Shoreditch, where the excitement is palpable and the atmosphere is electric. As you enter, you'll be greeted by a dozens of large HD screens broadcasting the latest games and matches from around the world. Our state-of-the-art sound system will transport you straight into the action as you soak up the energy and enthusiasm of fellow sports fans.



Fancy some friendly competition? We've got table football and a brand-new interactive dartboard with dozens of fun game modes. Team up with your mates or go head-to-head to crown a winner!
